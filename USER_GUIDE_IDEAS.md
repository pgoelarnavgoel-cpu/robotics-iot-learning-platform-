# User Guide: Submit Your Project Ideas

## Overview
Share your robotics and IoT project ideas with the community! This feature allows you to contribute your own project concepts, which will be reviewed by admins before being published.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- You must be logged in to submit ideas
- If you don't have an account, sign up first

### Accessing the Feature
1. Log in to your account
2. Click the **"Submit Idea"** button in the header (bright blue button)
3. Or navigate to "My Ideas" and click "Submit New Idea"

## Submitting an Idea

### Step 1: Fill in Project Details
Complete all required fields:

1. **Project Title** *
   - Give your project a clear, descriptive name
   - Example: "Smart Home Automation System"

2. **Category** *
   - Choose: Robotics or IoT

3. **Difficulty Level** *
   - Beginner: Simple projects for newcomers
   - Intermediate: Moderate complexity
   - Advanced: Complex projects requiring experience

4. **Project Overview** *
   - Brief description of your project
   - What does it do?

5. **Required Components** *
   - List all hardware components needed
   - Separate with commas
   - Example: "Arduino Uno, DHT11 Sensor, LED, Breadboard"

6. **Step-by-Step Instructions** *
   - Detailed guide to build the project
   - Include all necessary steps

7. **How It Works** *
   - Explain the working principle
   - Technical details

8. **Real-World Applications** *
   - Where can this be used?
   - Practical use cases

### Step 2: Choose Payment Option

If you don't have an active subscription, you'll see a payment dialog with two options:

#### Option 1: One-Time Payment
- **Price**: ₹50 per idea
- **Best for**: Occasional submissions
- Pay only when you submit

#### Option 2: Monthly Subscription
- **Price**: ₹150/month
- **Best for**: Regular contributors
- Submit unlimited ideas
- No additional payment per idea

### Step 3: Complete Payment
1. Select your preferred payment option
2. Click "Proceed to Payment"
3. Payment will be processed (simulated in demo)
4. Your idea will be submitted for review

## Managing Your Ideas

### View Your Submissions
1. Click **"My Ideas"** in the header
2. See all your submitted ideas with their status

### Understanding Status

#### 🟡 Pending
- Your idea is awaiting admin review
- No action needed from you
- Check back later for updates

#### 🟢 Approved
- Congratulations! Your idea is now public
- It will appear in the projects section
- Other users can view and learn from it

#### 🔴 Rejected
- Your idea was not approved
- You'll see a rejection reason explaining why
- You can submit a revised version

### Statistics Dashboard
View your submission statistics:
- **Total Ideas**: All submissions
- **Approved**: Successfully published ideas
- **Pending Review**: Awaiting admin decision

### Filter Your Ideas
Use tabs to filter by status:
- **All**: View everything
- **Pending**: Only pending submissions
- **Approved**: Only approved ideas
- **Rejected**: Only rejected submissions

## Subscription Benefits

### Subscription Status

  - Submit Idea page (top right)

## Tips for Success

### Writing Great Ideas
1. **Be Clear**: Use simple, understandable language
2. **Be Detailed**: Include all necessary information
3. **Be Practical**: Ensure components are commonly available
4. **Be Original**: Share unique or innovative concepts

### Increasing Approval Chances
- ✅ Complete all fields thoroughly
- ✅ Provide accurate component lists
- ✅ Write clear, step-by-step instructions
- ✅ Explain practical applications
- ✅ Check spelling and grammar
- ❌ Avoid incomplete information
- ❌ Don't copy existing projects
- ❌ Don't submit unsafe projects

## Common Questions

### How long does review take?
- Review time depends on admin availability
- Check "My Ideas" regularly for updates

### Can I edit submitted ideas?
- Currently, editing is not available
- If rejected, submit a new, improved version

### What happens after approval?
- Your idea becomes public
- It appears in the projects section
- Other users can view and learn from it

### Can I cancel my subscription?
- Contact an admin for subscription management
- Existing submissions remain unaffected

### What if my idea is rejected?
- Read the rejection reason carefully
- Address the issues mentioned
- Submit a revised version
